Salmon Recipe

Description

This is my favorite recipe ever!! It's very simple to make and incredibly delicious. It uses a combination of sauces as marinade, which makes it incredibly flavorful and delicious! It takes about an hour to marinade and 15 minutes to cook, so it's very little work!

Recommended Sides

  • Rice
  • Mashed Potatoes

Ingedients

  • Salmon Filets
  • 1/2 cup Kikkoman Soy Sauce
  • 6 Garlic Cloves, minced
  • 1/4 cup rice vinegar
  • 1/4 cup cane vinegar
  • 1/2 tsp dark soy sauce
  • 1/2 tsp light soy sauce
  • 1 drop ginger paste

Steps

Marinade

  1. Preheat oven to 350 F
  2. Put salmon in marinating bag
  3. Combine the soy sauces, rice vinegar, and cane vinegar
  4. Mince garlic, and take drop of ginger paste and combine with liquids
  5. Pour liquids into salmon bag
  6. Marinade for 1 hour, flipping every 15 minutes

Baking

  1. Remove salmon from bag, place them on baking sheet
  2. Put baking sheet in oven, let salmon cook for 10 minutes
  3. Switch oven to broil on high for 3 minutes
  4. Remove salmon and enjoy!
